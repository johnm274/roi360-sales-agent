# Account Growth — Expanding and Protecting Existing Clients

## The Growth Mindset

Every live storefront is a platform for expansion. The initial deployment is just the beginning. Your goal after go-live: more templates, more users, more departments, more products, and a stickier relationship.

## Expansion Triggers

Watch for these signals that a client is ready to grow:

### Usage-Based Triggers
- **High template usage** — if a template is being ordered frequently, there are likely similar materials that could be templated too
- **Users requesting materials not on the platform** — "Can we get our exhibition banners on here too?"
- **Multiple departments asking for access** — HR, Sales, Operations all want their own templates
- **Seasonal spikes** — events, campaigns, recruitment drives create demand for new templates

### Relationship-Based Triggers
- **New locations opening** — each new branch/franchisee needs materials
- **Brand refresh** — new logos, colours, guidelines = all templates need updating (billable work)
- **New product launches** — new products need new marketing materials
- **Staff changes** — new marketing manager wants to make their mark, modernise processes
- **Merger / acquisition** — integrating brands, standardising materials

### Commercial Triggers
- **Contract renewal** — perfect time to review, expand, and re-price
- **Budget cycle** — new budget year, new priorities, new investment
- **Competitor activity** — if a competitor offers something similar, add more value to stay ahead

## Cross-Sell Opportunities

### Template Types to Add Over Time
1. Start: Business cards, letterheads, basic leaflets
2. Add: Posters, banners, social media templates
3. Expand: Presentations, proposals, email signatures
4. Advanced: Variable data campaigns, direct mail, personalised print

### Feature Upgrades
- **Approval workflows** — as usage grows, clients need more control
- **SSO integration** — makes access easier, increases adoption
- **Advanced reporting** — management wants visibility
- **API integration** — connect to CRM, ERP, or marketing systems
- **Content databases** — centralised data (pricing, addresses, team details) feeding into templates

### Service Add-Ons
- **Template design service** — ongoing template creation as a managed service
- **Training sessions** — for new starters, new departments
- **Quarterly reviews** — formal review of usage, value, and expansion opportunities
- **Brand audit** — review all their materials and identify what could be templated

## Rescuing Under-Used Storefronts

If a client's storefront is going unused, act quickly:

### Diagnosis Questions
1. Are users aware the platform exists? (Often they're not)
2. Are the templates relevant to what people actually need?
3. Is the login process easy? (SSO makes a huge difference)
4. Is there an internal champion pushing adoption?
5. Has anyone been trained on how to use it?

### Rescue Actions
1. **Re-launch** — treat it as a fresh launch with communications, training, and quick wins
2. **Refresh templates** — update or add templates that match current needs
3. **Simplify access** — implement SSO, send direct links, add to intranet
4. **Find a champion** — identify someone internally who will push adoption
5. **Set targets** — agree usage targets with the client and track together
6. **Show value** — pull usage data and demonstrate what's been achieved, even if small

## Quarterly Review Agenda

Run quarterly reviews with active clients:

1. **Usage review** (10 mins) — orders, users, templates used, trends
2. **Value delivered** (10 mins) — time saved, cost avoided, brand consistency
3. **What's working** (5 mins) — celebrate wins, highlight power users
4. **What could be better** (10 mins) — gaps, requested templates, user feedback
5. **Growth plan** (10 mins) — next templates, new departments, upcoming needs
6. **Roadmap preview** (5 mins) — what's coming in the platform (only confirmed features)

## Metrics to Track Per Client

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|------------------|
| Monthly orders | Is usage growing or declining? |
| Active users | How many people actually use it? |
| Template usage spread | Are they using all templates or just one? |
| Order turnaround | How fast are orders being completed? |
| Approval bottlenecks | Are approvals slowing things down? |
| Support tickets | Are users struggling with anything? |
| Print revenue from portal | Is the platform driving print business? |
